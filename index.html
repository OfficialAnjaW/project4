Whale, Hello There!

Hello whale friends! My name is Wendy, and I am a blue whale. I am a marine mammal. Some of my blue whale friends are up to 98 feet long and weigh up to 173 tons. We are the largest animals known to have ever existed. How crazy is that? I am excited to introduce you to some of my more distant friends. There’s Willie, who is an orca. Sometimes, he’s also known as a killer while, but he’s really a big old teddy bear. Then, there’s Wanda. She’s a humpback whale. She’s literally always on the jump. And finally, I want you to meet Wade. He’s a sperm whale. He can be a little shy sometimes. Whale, what are you waiting for? Keep on swimming to meet my friends!
