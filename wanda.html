Going in for the Krill!

Oh y’all! Wendy mentioned that you might stop by. My name is Wanda, and I’m a humpback whale. As you might see, I have a very distinctive body shape. I have long pectoral fins and a knobbly head. My friends and I love to make big waves when we breach out of the water. All of those people on the boats love it when we do that. Make sure to check out the pictures of our best jumps below. Our male friends like to sing long, complex songs, which can last 10-20 minutes They sometimes do that for hours and hours at a time. It can get a little much after a while. Besides singing and jumping, we also love to travel. We cover about 16,000 miles every year. We are going all the way from polar waters, where we feed, to more tropical waters, where we breed. It was nice talking to y’all, but I gotta jump!
